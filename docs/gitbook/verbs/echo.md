# Echo (Repost)

`Echo` is the verb for reposting or amplifying another user's post without additional commentary.

## Mechanics

- **Kind**: `REACTION_ECHO`.
- **History**: Recorded in the actor's history log, allowing followers to see what they are amplifying.
- **State**: Like Spark, it supports `ADD` and `REMOVE` states.
- **Amplification**: Indexers use Echo events to calculate the reach and popularity of posts.
